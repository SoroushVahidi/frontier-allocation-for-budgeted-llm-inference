# Faithful Baseline Claim Language

## Allowed now (before rerun)
- "We evaluate faithful S1-style, TALE-EP-style, and fair L1-style baseline variants under our matched local harness."
- "These are adapter/fairness-improved variants, not yet full official reproductions."

## Allowed after rerun (if results hold)
- "Our method outperforms our faithful S1-style and TALE-EP-style adapters under matched conditions."
- "Our fair L1 comparator is exceeded under matched settings."

## Still forbidden
- "We beat official TALE/S1/L1 baselines" (until strict official parity is demonstrated).
- "We faithfully reproduce TALE-PT or full official S1 stack" (not implemented here).
