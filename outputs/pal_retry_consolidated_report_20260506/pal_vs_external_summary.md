# PAL vs external_l1_max (100-case paired)

From the fresh 100-case paired run and statistical analysis:

- `external_l1_max`: **75/100**
- `PAL`: **80/100**
- PAL delta: **+5 percentage points**
- Discordants: **PAL-only 10**, **external-only 5**
- P-value: **0.3018**
- Bootstrap 95% CI for (PAL - external): **-2 pp to +13 pp**

Conclusion: PAL is competitive and directionally better on this sample, but evidence is **not statistically decisive** for superiority.
