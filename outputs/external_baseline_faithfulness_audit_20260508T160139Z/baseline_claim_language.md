# Baseline Claim Language

## A. Conservative wording allowed now
- "We compare against our implemented L1-style, TALE-style, and S1-style external baselines under matched local conditions."
- "Our integrated method outperforms each individual external-style baseline on this pilot."

## B. Strong wording NOT allowed yet
- "We faithfully reproduce official TALE/S1/L1 results."
- "We beat the official TALE/S1/L1 baselines." 

## C. Strong wording allowed only after additional work
- "We reproduce official S1/TALE/L1 baselines" only after verified checkpoint, prompt, decoding, budget-unit, and harness parity plus reruns.

## D. How to describe best_external oracle
- "best_external is an oracle upper bound that picks the best external-style baseline per case; it is not a deployable single baseline method."

## E. Pilot-scaffolded runtime caveat
- "Integrated method results are from a pilot-scaffolded runtime and should be interpreted separately from full production-runtime-equivalent claims."
