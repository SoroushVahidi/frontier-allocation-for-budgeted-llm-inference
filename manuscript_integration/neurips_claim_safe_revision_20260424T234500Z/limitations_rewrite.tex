\\paragraph{Limitations.}
Our evidence does not support a frontier-family dominance claim over \\texttt{external\\_l1\\_max} in combined OpenAI+Cohere real-model audits; outcomes are mixed and often statistically fragile. Method ranking is surface-sensitive: winner identity can change with dataset, budget, and evidence layer. Real-model results are therefore treated as appendix-level robustness corroboration rather than headline proof. In addition, while the operational controller is reproducible and deterministic at implementation level, several gates are heuristic/state-dependent (e.g., coverage forcing and family-cap logic) rather than globally optimal closed-form control. Finally, held-out analyses include dry-run scaffolds and partial slices; fully exhaustive held-out runs may still be required before making broader external-validity claims.
