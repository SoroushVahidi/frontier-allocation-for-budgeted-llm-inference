\title{Failure-Trace-Guided Gold-Free Policy Gating for Budgeted LLM Reasoning\\
\large Cohere Matched-Budget Evidence on Disjoint 720-Example Evaluation}

\begin{abstract}
We present a journal-ready evidence package for a gold-free policy gating method (FIX-2+FIX-4) that improves matched-budget LLM reasoning accuracy in the tested Cohere setting. On a disjoint 720-example aggregate, FIX-2+FIX-4 achieves 80.69\% versus L1 77.64\%, S1 77.08\%, and TALE 75.14\%, with positive source-stratified confidence-interval lower bounds for all baseline comparisons. We provide reproducible rule definitions, ablations, cost/budget summaries, and conservative claim boundaries.
\keywords{Large language models \and budgeted inference \and reproducibility \and policy gating}
\end{abstract}
